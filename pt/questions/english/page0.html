Database

The following features describes the behavior of a fictitious bees society and its database (on the left):

  • Each bee have only one job
  • The age of the bees is measured in days
  • The bees are paid with honey by its job
  • The salary of each bee is associated with its job
  • On the society, the job determines the hierarchy of each bee, in wich the higher position is the queen
  • Each bee live on a honeycomb that can shelter many bees
  • A beehive can be located by your x and y position

Your job as a system analyst at the service of the bees is to provide informations about the hives and its management
